This next step in the study involves providing information from your web browser about how you use web search engines and how you use the web overall. This information will be based on your web browser's history, and it will include when and how you ran searches as well as counts of how many websites and webpages you visited over the past 30 days.

Your privacy and data security are of the utmost importance to us. We have implemented technical safeguards to prevent us from collecting your search queries, the other websites or webpages that you have visited, or browser information that identifies you. You will have an opportunity to preview your information before you provide it. You may also review a detailed description of the types of data that we will collect here.

Please continue for step-by-step directions on how to provide this information from your web browser.
